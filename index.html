I am an Assistant Professor (RTD-B) in the Department of Mathematics of the University of Bologna.

My main research interest is the numerical analysis of (nonlinear) partial differential equations in materials science and engineering, in particular in liquid crystal theory, micromagnetism and spintronics. I am also interested in fundamental questions in the analysis of finite element methods and in (goal-oriented) adaptivity for stochastic Galerkin finite element methods in uncertainty quantification and for model order reduction methods.

For general updates about me, please refer to my Twitter account.
