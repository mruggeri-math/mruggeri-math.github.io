News

I have a Twitter account, on which I used to post some updates about my work (recent publications, teaching highlights, open positions, participation in scientific events, etc.). I am not happy with the recent evolution of this social network (from Twitter to X), so I have decided to discontinue my account (which will be deleted at some point) and post all news on this webpage.

2024

  • February 12, 2024: This week I am attending the workshop on Numerical Analysis of Multiphysics Problems at ICERM.

  • February 10, 2024: A new preprint is available on arXiv. My colleague and friend Gregor Gantner (University of Bonn) and I propose an adaptive hybrid FEM-BEM method to solve full-space linear transmission problems and show its convergence with optimal rates. The paper can be downloaded from this link.

  • February 5, 2024: This week and the next I am visiting the Institute for Computational and Experimental Research in Mathematics (ICERM) to participate in some of the activities of the Semester Program on Numerical PDEs: Analysis, Algorithms, and Data Challenges.

  • January 25, 2024: Today and tomorrow I am at Ruhr University Bochum, to participate in the 23rd GAMM Seminar on Microstructures. Tomorrow at 11 am CET I will give a keynote talk, in which I will present some recent results on the numerical analysis of a model of antiferromagnetic and ferrimagnetic materials.

  • January 18, 2024: Today I will attend some talks of the workshop on Sketching, Mixed Precision, and Associated Algorithms for Scientific Computing, organized by my colleague Davide Palitta in our department.
